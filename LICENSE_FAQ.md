SaferTrade Licensing FAQ (BUSL-1.1)

Q: Can I use SaferTrade for free?
A: Yes, for non-production use: learning, development, testing, research, internal PoCs.

Q: Can I host SaaS or sell it?
A: Not without a commercial license before December 10, 2029. BUSL-1.1 prohibits production use.

Q: What happens on the Change Date?
A: The code automatically converts to GPLv2 or compatible open source license.

Q: Can I contribute?
A: Yes, contributions are welcome under the repository's Contributor License Agreement (CLA) if applicable; otherwise under BUSL-1.1 terms.

Q: Why BUSL vs. MIT?
A: BUSL prevents direct commercial competition while enabling community collaboration and adoption.

Q: How do I get a commercial license?
A: See COMMERCIAL_LICENSE.md or contact licensing@safertrade.io.
